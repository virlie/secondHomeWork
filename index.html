Ping Pong

Number counter and replacer

This form will count from 1 to the number you input, changing certain values.

Any number it encounters that is divisble by 3 will become "PING"

Any number it encounters that is divisble by 5 will become "PONG"

Any number it encounters that is divisble by 15 will become "PINGPONG"
